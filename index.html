About Me


Joe Gartman is the Founder of Redeemed Royalty; a Christian clothing brand/ministry. Upon giving his life to Jesus in 2011, Joe saw a need to inspire others to share their testimony. Redeemed Royalty was birthed in hopes of doing just that. Now, after years of trying to get the brand going, in 2020, he finally launched. Joe also wanted to help fund this journey, so he decided to finish his degree in computer science. He is now completing the Full Stack Web Development course at Bethel School of Technology.
